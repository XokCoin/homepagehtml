What is a NFT?

NFT stands for "Non-Fungible Token" and is the technical term for an unique, one of a kind digital token.
‍
The NFT is stored on the crypto blockchain called "Ethereum", and is self-governed by a 'smart contract'.

A crypto wallet is required to stored the NFT.
We recommend Metamask wallet (Chrome Browser extension - metamask.io).

How to get a Shabu Town Shiba NFT?

You can obtain a Shabu Town Shiba by purchasing a Shabu Crate. Connect your Metamask (or other) wallet to the website to swap Ethereum for Shabu Crate.

What can I do with Shabu Town NFTs?

Unlike many other NFTs, Shabu Town NFTs provides utility at launch by integrating with an online community platform, Shabu Town.
‍
Initially you will be able to use your Shabu Town Shiba NFT as your avatar on Shabu Town, but more utility will be added with further development.
